Talk2Me

Advice Your Way

In the modern world one is surrounded, indeed inundated, by information. Long gone are the days where finding the information you needed was the hardest part of learning. We live in an information saturated world, and it can be hard to decide which information is the right fit for your goals. How does one deal with this problem?

Enter Talk2Me a website platform geared towards giving YOU the advice you are looking for! On the other end of the spectrum it is a platform that connects YOU to others in need of the knowledge you possess on both personal and professional levels. Hazzah.

Allows for closer relationships between people of all ages and backgrounds around the world

Ability to posts questions public or private (provides fields for title, question And question type.)

Tab to let users review their posted questions edit/delete them

Mentor tab for users to see who has offered to give them advice

Advisory feed tab for displaying public questions and allow advisor to select them in order to give advice through the mentor tab
